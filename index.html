Sample Menu Concepts

Real Food, Balanced For You

See how traditional Pakistani cuisine is structured in our diet programs.

🌸 Sample PCOS Friendly Day

Focus: High protein, low glycemic index, anti-inflammatory whole foods.

  • Breakfast: 2 Boiled Eggs + 1 Multigrain Roti or Oats porridge with seeds.
  • Lunch: Daal / Chicken Chana Salan + Bran Roti + Fresh Salad bowl.
  • Snack: Green Tea + Handful of roasted almonds/walnuts.
  • Dinner: Grilled Fish or Chicken Tikka + Steamed Sabzi (Vegetables).

🥗 Sample Calorie-Deficit Day

Focus: High volume, high satiety to keep you full longer.

  • Breakfast: Vegetable Omelette + 1 Slice Whole Wheat Toast + Black Tea/Coffee.
  • Lunch: 1 Small Portion Brown/Basmati Rice + Mix Sabzi Salan + Cucumber Raita.
  • Snack: Seasonal Fruit (Apple/Guava) or 1 Cup Chana Chaat.
  • Dinner: 1 Small Bran Roti + Bhindi/Palak Salan + Chicken Breasts.

🩸 Sample Glycemic-Control Day

Focus: Fiber-rich carbohydrate pairing to prevent glucose spikes.

  • Breakfast: Whole Wheat Roti + Egg Bhurji + Sugar-free Cardamom Tea.
  • Lunch: Lentil Soup (Daal) + Barley/Whole Grain Roti + Kachumbar Salad.
  • Snack: Roasted Chickpeas (Bhuna Chana) + Cinnamon Green Tea.
  • Dinner: Grilled Kebabs + Fresh Salad + Apple Cider Vinegar Shot before food.
